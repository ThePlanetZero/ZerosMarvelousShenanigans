The Game
Zero's Marvelous Shenanigans is a multiplayer Roblox fighting game where you don't just choose abilities — you build them. Using the in-game Graph Editor, you can connect nodes together to create your own attacks, movement abilities, projectiles, effects, and much more. Once your moveset is ready, take it into battle against other players and see whose creations come out on top.

Multiplayer
Yes! Battles are built around multiplayer. Fight your friends, challenge other players, and test your custom movesets against abilities created by everyone else.
At launch, the primary focus is player-versus-player combat. Additional experiences and game modes may be introduced in future updates.
Progress & Saving
Yes. Your progress is automatically saved, including your abilities, customization, and other important data, so you can continue where you left off.
Access & Devices
Release includes full support for PC. Mobile support is currently in development and will arrive in a future update. Console support is planned after that.
