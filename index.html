Developer Notes
From Zero.

When I first started this project, I just wanted to make a fun fighting game. Somewhere along the way, it turned into something much bigger.

One of my favorite parts of making Zero's Marvelous Shenanigans has been building the graph editor. Instead of choosing from a list of abilities, you get to create your own. I love seeing ideas that I never would've thought of myself come to life because someone connected a few nodes in a weird way.

The game is still growing, and there's a lot I want to build. Every update teaches me something new, and every bit of feedback helps shape where the project goes next.

Thanks for stopping by, whether you're here to throw punches, build something ridiculous, or just look around. I hope you have as much fun playing as I've had making it.

Sometimes the best ideas come from connecting things that were never meant to be connected.

— Zero
